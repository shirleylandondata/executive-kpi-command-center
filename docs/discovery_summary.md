# Discovery Summary

## Business Challenge

Northstar Analytics has grown rapidly, but its executive reporting process has not scaled with the business.

Revenue, sales, customer, and product information is maintained across separate systems. Leadership relies on manually consolidated spreadsheets that require several hours to prepare and may contain inconsistent KPI definitions.

## Proposed Solution

The Executive KPI Command Center will create one standardized reporting environment for the executive team.

The solution will combine revenue, customer, sales, and product data into a documented analytics model. Power BI will provide executive dashboards, while an AI-assisted workflow will generate a concise performance briefing.

## Expected Business Value

The solution is designed to:

- Reduce manual reporting work
- Improve KPI consistency
- Provide earlier visibility into customer and revenue risk
- Support executive decision-making
- Demonstrate a scalable approach to business intelligence

## Discovery Outputs

Phase 1 produced:

- A project charter
- Stakeholder requirements
- Business requirements
- KPI definitions
- Success measures
- Dashboard scope

## Next Phase

Phase 2 will define the source systems, datasets, entity relationships, star schema, data dictionary, and solution architecture.
