# KPI Dictionary

## Annual Recurring Revenue

### Abbreviation

ARR

### Business Definition

The annualized value of active recurring subscription revenue.

### Calculation

ARR = Monthly Recurring Revenue × 12

### Business Owner

Chief Financial Officer

### Reporting Frequency

Daily and monthly

### Decision Supported

Determines whether recurring revenue is growing at the rate required to meet company targets.

---

## Monthly Recurring Revenue

### Abbreviation

MRR

### Business Definition

The normalized monthly value of active subscription contracts.

### Calculation

MRR = Sum of monthly subscription values for active customers

### Business Owner

Chief Financial Officer

### Reporting Frequency

Daily and monthly

### Decision Supported

Tracks recurring revenue changes and supports short-term revenue forecasting.

---

## Net Revenue Retention

### Abbreviation

NRR

### Business Definition

The percentage of recurring revenue retained from existing customers after expansion, contraction, and churn.

### Calculation

NRR =  
Beginning MRR + Expansion MRR - Contraction MRR - Churned MRR  
divided by Beginning MRR

### Business Owner

Chief Financial Officer and Vice President of Customer Success

### Reporting Frequency

Monthly

### Decision Supported

Measures whether the existing customer base is growing or shrinking.

---

## Gross Revenue Retention

### Abbreviation

GRR

### Business Definition

The percentage of recurring revenue retained from existing customers before expansion revenue.

### Calculation

GRR =  
Beginning MRR - Contraction MRR - Churned MRR  
divided by Beginning MRR

### Business Owner

Vice President of Customer Success

### Reporting Frequency

Monthly

### Decision Supported

Measures the company's ability to retain existing customer revenue.

---

## Customer Churn Rate

### Business Definition

The percentage of active customers lost during a reporting period.

### Calculation

Customer Churn Rate =  
Customers Lost During Period divided by Customers at Beginning of Period

### Business Owner

Vice President of Customer Success

### Reporting Frequency

Monthly

### Decision Supported

Identifies changes in customer retention.

---

## Pipeline Coverage

### Business Definition

The value of the open sales pipeline compared with the revenue target.

### Calculation

Pipeline Coverage = Open Pipeline Value divided by Remaining Revenue Target

### Business Owner

Chief Revenue Officer

### Reporting Frequency

Daily and weekly

### Decision Supported

Determines whether enough sales opportunity exists to achieve the revenue target.

---

## Win Rate

### Business Definition

The percentage of closed sales opportunities that were won.

### Calculation

Win Rate = Won Opportunities divided by Total Closed Opportunities

### Business Owner

Chief Revenue Officer

### Reporting Frequency

Weekly and monthly

### Decision Supported

Evaluates sales effectiveness and supports forecasting.

---

## Product Adoption Rate

### Business Definition

The percentage of active customer accounts that use a defined set of core product features.

### Calculation

Product Adoption Rate =  
Accounts Meeting Adoption Threshold divided by Active Accounts

### Business Owner

Vice President of Product

### Reporting Frequency

Weekly and monthly

### Decision Supported

Measures whether customers are receiving value from the product.

---

## Customer Health Score

### Business Definition

A composite score used to estimate the overall health and retention risk of a customer account.

### Proposed Components

- Product usage
- Feature adoption
- Support ticket activity
- Subscription status
- Payment status
- Renewal proximity

### Business Owner

Vice President of Customer Success

### Reporting Frequency

Daily

### Decision Supported

Prioritizes customer outreach and identifies accounts requiring intervention.
