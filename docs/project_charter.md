# Project Charter

## Project Name

Executive KPI Command Center

## Client

Northstar Analytics

## Industry

B2B Software as a Service

## Project Sponsor

Chief Executive Officer

## Project Lead

Shirley Landon  
Business Intelligence and AI Operations Consultant

## Business Problem

Northstar Analytics relies on separate reporting systems across sales, finance, customer success, marketing, and product.

Executives receive performance information through spreadsheets, exports, and manually prepared reports. This process requires several hours each week and creates inconsistent KPI definitions across departments.

Leadership does not have one trusted view of company performance.

## Project Objective

Design an Executive KPI Command Center that consolidates business data into one reporting environment and gives leadership timely visibility into revenue, customers, sales, and product performance.

## Business Outcomes

- Reduce manual executive reporting time
- Standardize KPI definitions
- Improve visibility into company performance
- Identify revenue and customer risks earlier
- Support faster executive decision-making
- Create an automated executive briefing process

## Project Scope

The project will cover:

- Revenue performance
- Recurring revenue
- Customer retention
- Customer health
- Sales pipeline
- Sales conversion
- Product adoption
- Executive KPI reporting
- AI-generated performance summaries

## Out of Scope

The first version will not include:

- Payroll analytics
- Employee performance analytics
- Full financial accounting
- Real-time production integrations
- Personally identifiable customer data
- Automated business decisions without human review

## Primary Deliverables

- Executive KPI dashboard
- Revenue dashboard
- Customer dashboard
- Sales dashboard
- Product dashboard
- SQL analytics model
- KPI dictionary
- Data dictionary
- Architecture diagram
- AI executive briefing
- GitHub documentation
- Loom walkthrough
