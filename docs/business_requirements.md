# Business Requirements

## BR-01: Centralized Executive Reporting

The solution must provide one consolidated view of company performance across revenue, sales, customers, and product usage.

## BR-02: Standardized KPI Definitions

The solution must use consistent definitions for all executive KPIs.

## BR-03: Historical Trend Analysis

Users must be able to compare current performance with prior months, quarters, and years.

## BR-04: Performance Targets

The dashboard must compare actual results with business targets.

## BR-05: Executive Summary

The dashboard must provide a concise summary of major performance changes, risks, and opportunities.

## BR-06: Drill-Through Analysis

Executives must be able to move from high-level KPIs to supporting department and account-level detail.

## BR-07: Customer Risk Identification

The solution must identify customers with declining usage, upcoming renewals, or other churn indicators.

## BR-08: Revenue Movement Analysis

The solution must distinguish between:

- New revenue
- Expansion revenue
- Contraction revenue
- Churned revenue

## BR-09: Sales Funnel Visibility

The solution must show conversion rates and pipeline values across sales stages.

## BR-10: Product Adoption Analysis

The solution must measure product usage and feature adoption across customer segments.

## BR-11: Data Refresh

The portfolio version will simulate a daily refresh process.

## BR-12: Data Quality Monitoring

The solution must identify missing, duplicate, invalid, or inconsistent records.

## BR-13: Usability

The dashboard must be understandable to executive users without technical training.

## BR-14: Documentation

The project must include documentation for KPI definitions, data sources, transformations, assumptions, and limitations.

## BR-15: Security and Privacy

The portfolio project must use fictional data and must not contain confidential or personally identifiable information.
