# Dashboard Scope

## Page 1: Executive Overview

### Purpose

Provide a concise view of total company performance.

### KPIs

- ARR
- MRR
- Revenue Growth
- NRR
- Customer Churn
- Pipeline Coverage
- Product Adoption
- At-Risk Customers

### Visuals

- KPI cards
- Monthly revenue trend
- Customer health distribution
- Sales pipeline summary
- Product adoption trend
- Risk and opportunity panel

---

## Page 2: Revenue Performance

### Purpose

Explain changes in recurring revenue.

### KPIs

- Beginning MRR
- New MRR
- Expansion MRR
- Contraction MRR
- Churned MRR
- Ending MRR
- ARR
- Average Revenue Per Account

### Visuals

- MRR movement waterfall
- ARR trend
- Revenue by segment
- Revenue by plan
- New versus expansion revenue

---

## Page 3: Customer Health

### Purpose

Identify customer retention risks and expansion opportunities.

### KPIs

- Active Customers
- Churn Rate
- Renewal Rate
- Customer Health Score
- At-Risk Customers
- Expansion Candidates

### Visuals

- Health score distribution
- Renewal calendar
- At-risk account table
- Churn by segment
- Engagement versus retention

Show whether the reporting data is complete and trustworthy.

### Visuals

- Quality scorecard
- Errors by source
- Validation history
