# Stakeholder Analysis

## Chief Executive Officer

### Primary Questions

- Is the company on track to meet its annual revenue target?
- Which areas of the business require immediate attention?
- Are customer retention and product adoption improving?
- What risks could affect growth?

### Required KPIs

- Annual Recurring Revenue
- Monthly Recurring Revenue
- Net Revenue Retention
- Gross Revenue Retention
- Customer Churn Rate
- Pipeline Coverage
- Product Adoption Rate
- Revenue Growth Rate

### Dashboard Needs

The CEO requires a high-level view with trend indicators, alerts, and the ability to identify the source of major performance changes.

---

## Chief Financial Officer

### Primary Questions

- How predictable is recurring revenue?
- How much revenue has been lost through churn?
- What is the expected revenue forecast?
- Which customer segments generate the highest value?

### Required KPIs

- Annual Recurring Revenue
- Monthly Recurring Revenue
- Expansion Revenue
- Churned Revenue
- Average Revenue Per Account
- Revenue Forecast
- Customer Lifetime Value

### Dashboard Needs

The CFO requires financial trends, recurring revenue movements, customer value analysis, and forecast comparisons.

---

## Chief Revenue Officer

### Primary Questions

- Is the sales team on track to meet quota?
- Where are prospects dropping out of the funnel?
- Is there enough pipeline to meet future targets?
- Which sales channels perform best?

### Required KPIs

- Pipeline Value
- Pipeline Coverage
- Win Rate
- Sales Conversion Rate
- Average Deal Size
- Sales Cycle Length
- Quota Attainment

### Dashboard Needs

The CRO requires pipeline visibility, rep performance, stage conversion, and sales forecasting.

---

## Vice President of Customer Success

### Primary Questions

- Which customers are at risk of churning?
- How many renewals are due?
- Which accounts have expansion potential?
- How does product engagement affect retention?

### Required KPIs

- Customer Churn Rate
- Logo Retention
- Net Revenue Retention
- Customer Health Score
- Renewal Rate
- Expansion Revenue
- Support Activity

### Dashboard Needs

The VP of Customer Success requires account-level drill-through, health scoring, renewal tracking, and churn indicators.

---

## Vice President of Product

### Primary Questions

- Are customers adopting the product?
- Which features create the most engagement?
- How does usage differ across customer segments?
- Does product adoption relate to renewals?

### Required KPIs

- Active Users
- Product Adoption Rate
- Feature Adoption
- Usage Frequency
- Engagement Score
- Dormant Accounts

### Dashboard Needs

The VP of Product requires product usage trends, feature-level analysis, and links between engagement and customer retention.
